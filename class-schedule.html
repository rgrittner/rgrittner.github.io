Class Schedule

Session Starts June 9


We will be offering the following classes during this session. This is a 4 week session. All classes meet in Renee's backyard, located near Cottage Grove unless otherwise stated. An exact address will be proved upon registration.

Unless othersie stated, all classes are $100 for a 4 week session

Intro to Rally
10:00 am
This class is for dogs who have good leash skills, a solid sit, down, and a short stay. We will continue to work on heeling, and developing rhythm between dog and handler. Short courses will be set up for students to work on during class, and different signs will be assigend as homework.

Basic Manners
11:30 am
Our basic manners class is suitable for dogs of all ages who are comfortable on leash around other dogs. This class will cover leash skills, sit, down, stay & recall foundations. This class will also focus on building an excellent relationship between you and your dog.

Intro to Agility
2:00 pm
This class will begin your agility skills with foundations for end-contact behavior, value for following the handler, and will expose your dog to new surfaces.
